Frequently Asked Questions

Below are some generally frequently asked questions. If your question is not in here feel free to contact us.

Q: What is the festival about?
A: The festival is a celebration of music, arts, and culture. It's an event where people come together to enjoy live performances, food, and other activities.

Q: When and where is the festival being held?
A: Check the festival website Line-Up or all festivals for more information on the exact time and location.

Q: How can I buy tickets?
A: You can purchase tickets online through the festival website or at the box office on the day of the event. We recommend purchasing tickets in advance to avoid lines and ensure you get a spot.

Q: Can I bring my own food and drinks?
A: Outside food and drinks are not allowed at the festival. There will be a variety of food vendors and beverage stands available for purchase.

Q: What should I wear to the festival?
A: Dress comfortably and appropriately for the weather. We recommend wearing comfortable shoes as you'll be on your feet most of the day. Check the festival website for any specific dress codes or recommendations.

Q: Are children allowed at the festival?
A: Yes, children are welcome at the festival. However, some activities may be restricted to certain age groups, so check the festival website for more information.

Q: Can I bring my pet to the festival?
A: Pets are not allowed at the festival, with the exception of service animals.

Q: Is there parking available?
A: Yes, there is parking available at the festival. However, we encourage carpooling or taking public transportation to reduce traffic congestion.

Q: What happens if it rains?
A: The festival will go on rain or shine. However, in case of severe weather conditions, we may need to cancel or reschedule some activities. Check the festival website or social media pages for updates.

Q: Are there any prohibited items?
A: Yes, there are some prohibited items such as weapons, illegal drugs, and alcohol. Check the festival website for a full list of prohibited items.

We hope this FAQ helps you plan your visit to the festival. Have a great time!
