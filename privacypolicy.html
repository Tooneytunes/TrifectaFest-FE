Privacy Policy for TrifectaFest

General information

At TrifectaFest, we are committed to protecting your privacy and keeping your personal information safe.
This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you attend our festival.
 

What Information We Collect

We collect the following types of personal information when you attend our festival:

- Contact information, including your name, email address, phone number, and mailing address.
- Demographic information, such as your age, gender, and location.
- Payment information, including your credit card or other payment information.
- Information about your attendance at our festival, including the events you attended, the dates you attended, and your purchases.
 

How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

- To process your registration and attendance at our festival.
- To send you important updates and information about our festival, such as changes to our schedule or venue information.
- To send you marketing communications about our festival, including promotions, discounts, and upcoming events.
- To improve our festival and the services we offer.
 

How We Protect Your Information

- We take the security of your personal information seriously and use industry-standard practices to protect your data.
- We use physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.
 

Sharing Your Information

- We may share your personal information with third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf, such as payment processing, email marketing, and data analysis.
- We may also share your personal information with our sponsors, partners, or vendors who are involved in our festival.

- We will never sell your personal information to third parties.
 

Your Rights

- You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information that we hold.
- You can do so by contacting us using the information provided at the end of this privacy policy.

- You also have the right to opt-out of receiving marketing communications from us.
- You can do so by clicking the "unsubscribe" link at the bottom of our emails.
 

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on our website.
 

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact us at trifecta@fest.info.
 
Thank you for trusting us with your personal information.
We look forward to seeing you at TrifectaFest.
